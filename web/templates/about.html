What is the point? Why should you want a Smart-Cube?

What is a Smart-Cube?

Smart-Cubes are versatile cubes which you can use to manage your time and let your friends know what you're doing. Smart-Cubes are physical objects that you keep on your desk. Each Smart-Cube can be in one of 6 states, denoted by which side is face up. Simply change state by rotating the Smart-Cube!

Why should I use it?

The Smart-Cube will record how long was spent in each state and publish the information to this site. Using the Smart-Cube means you don't need to keep track of how long you've spent working on one project. Let the Smart-Cube keep track of it for you!
Once registered, the Smart-Cube will start recording which state it is in. You can apply a profile to your Smart-Cube which tells it what each state means. This website will display your previous states and various statistics about them at your leisure.

Is that all it does?

The Smart-Cube sends generic information to this website. It can be extended and interpretted by a seperate application very easily. Some example uses are:
  • A device to choose your music genre
  • A monitoring device for meeting rooms
  • And many more... It's up to you!
